## Versioning

Many instructions have deprecated v1 variants and current v2 variants. The v2 variants include support for farming rewards. Always use v2 variants unless you have a specific reason to use v1.

## Emergency Mode

Most instructions are disabled when the lending market is in emergency mode. Only administrative and refresh operations are allowed during emergency mode.
